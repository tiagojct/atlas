The work of folding a clinical tool or intervention into the existing clinical workflow — without disrupting it. The most common cause of CDSS failure is not algorithmic error, but failure of integration.

## Why it is the hardest problem

Clinical workflows are complex, poorly documented, locally variable, and defended by habit. A tool that adds 30 seconds to a 7-minute consultation is not a 30-second cost — it is a different consultation.

## Design principles

- Integrate into the existing tool (the EHR, not a separate app).
- Fire at decision points, not at login.
- Offer a default action, not just information.
- Stay silent when there is nothing to say.

## The test

Does the clinician use it when no one is watching? Usage data, not satisfaction surveys, reveal the quality of the integration.
