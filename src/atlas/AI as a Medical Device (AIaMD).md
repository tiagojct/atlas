The regulatory category for software that uses artificial intelligence and is intended to diagnose, treat, mitigate, or prevent disease — without being part of a physical medical device.

## Regulatory frameworks

- **FDA (US):** the Software as a Medical Device (SaMD) framework, with the Pre-Cert pilot for adaptive AI.
- **EU MDR (Europe):** Medical Device Regulation 2017/745, classifying AI under Rule 11. Notified bodies are still adapting to continuously-learning models.
- **IMDRF:** the International Medical Device Regulators Forum provides risk-categorisation frameworks.

## The adaptive challenge

Traditional regulation assumes a static device. AI models that learn continuously — updating weights from new data — break this assumption. The regulatory question is not *whether* the model will change, but how to assure safety *through* the change.
