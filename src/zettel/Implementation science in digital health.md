The study of methods for getting research findings into routine clinical practice. Implementation science asks not "does this intervention work?" but "how do we make it work in the real world?"

## Core frameworks

- **CFIR:** the Consolidated Framework for Implementation Research — five domains of barriers and facilitators.
- **RE-AIM:** Reach, Effectiveness, Adoption, Implementation, Maintenance.
- **Normalisation Process Theory (NPT):** how practices become routine, embedded, and sustained.

## The digital-health twist

Digital interventions bring implementation challenges of their own:

- They require infrastructure (connectivity, devices, IT support).
- They compete for attention in an already overloaded digital environment.
- They need updates and maintenance — implementation is not a one-off event.
